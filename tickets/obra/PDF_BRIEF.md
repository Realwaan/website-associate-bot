# Obra Marketplace Website

## Summary

A centralized digital marketplace for Filipino independent artists to showcase portfolios, manage commissions, and process local payments, eliminating foreign exchange fees and protecting against scams.

## Design System

- Logo: OBRA
- Fonts: Satoshi
- Color Palette: #154675, #6BB8FF, #0B2741, #FFFFFF, DARK GRAY, GRAY BLUE
- Visual Tone: Modern, vibrant, trustworthy, community‑focused
- Notes:
  - Use dark azure as primary brand color
  - Pastel azure for accents
  - Maintain high contrast for accessibility
  - Logo should be used in monochrome for dark backgrounds

## Pages

- **Home**
  - Purpose: Introduce the platform and encourage artists to join
  - Sections: Hero section, Feature highlights, CTA to sign up
  - Notes: Dark azure background, Pastel accents, Include hero illustration
- **Artist Dashboard**
  - Purpose: Allow artists to manage storefronts and commissions
  - Sections: Profile, Storefront editor, Commission waitlist, Payment settings
  - Notes: Responsive grid, Inline editing
- **Marketplace**
  - Purpose: Browse and discover artists
  - Sections: Search bar, Artist cards, Filters, Artist detail page
  - Notes: Card design uses pastel azure, Hover effects
- **Commission Management**
  - Purpose: Handle commission requests and agreements
  - Sections: Open requests, My commissions, Agreement editor
  - Notes: Secure messaging, Deadline reminders
- **Contact**
  - Purpose: Provide support and partnership inquiries
  - Sections: Contact form, Location map, FAQ
  - Notes: Mobile‑friendly, Accessible

## Features

- Artist storefront creation
